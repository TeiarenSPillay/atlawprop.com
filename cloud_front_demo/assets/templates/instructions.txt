To use these imports in your custom.less file, follow the below instrunctions:

Step 1:
Add the following to the your custom.less file:

@import url("../templates/your-desired-template-name-here.less");

Step 2:
Change "your-desired-template-name-here" to the name of your template. These
will correspond with the files in html/includes/

Step 3:
There is default styling for the templates. 

You can copy any class from this less file into your custom.less after your 
@import line and adjust the styles as desired to customize the look of these
templates.
